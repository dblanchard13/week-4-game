Lucy's Diamonds

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four diamonds above. By clicking on a diamond you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You help Lucy by matching your total score to the random number. However, if your total score goes over the random number, Lucy's health diminishes...

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
